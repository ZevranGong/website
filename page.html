ABOUT ME

This above all: to thine own self be true.

Previously on Ziyuan (Zevran) Gong...

Born in Changchun, China, I spent my leisurely childhood in the peaceful little city. When I was 15, I was fortunate to have the opportunity to study in one of the best high schools in Singapore under a national program. The transition prolonged my high school a little bit, but it was worth it to meet all the foreign-minds and embrace a globalized education. In a word, I was a lucky kid, but the thing I know about luck is: if you sit on it, luck will always run out.

Hence I chose to be a student double-majoring in mathematics (operations research) and computer science in Carnegie Mellon University, where courses are rigorous and hard work is rewarded. I developed my skills as a programmer, and my primary interests reside in distributed systems and computer game programming.

Aside from academia, I am also a casual PC gamer, but it seems I can't stay focused on a game for too long. I don't know whether it is a boon or bane, as this characteristic gives me a wide variety of gaming experiences in the limited time. My friends are often "shocked" by my sudden liveliness whenever PC gaming is involved.

On a sidenote, I used "Zevran", the name of an elven assassin character from Bioware's "Dragon Age: Origins" as my nickname, since it sounds like my hard-to-pronounce Chinese name (A more accurate pronunciation of my Chinese name can go as "Zee-one", but it is far less meaningful). I was hoping to simplify my name, but it doesn't seem to make everyone's life easier :)
